Can I get a refund

Can I get a refund?

Edited

Well, no. Unless...

There are a few key areas in our Terms of Service regarding refunds. We thought we'd make them easy to find, and offer an explanation for each mention.

From Your Registration Obligations

If we discontinue the Service, we will refund you any pre-paid fees on a pro rata basis, based upon the number of months left in the pre-payment period.

This indicates that we will offer a refund in cases where we need to take action by discontinuing service. As noted, the refund will be prorated based on the remaining subscription duration.

From Refunds and Cancellations

Payments made by you hereunder are final and non-refundable, unless otherwise determined by Suno.

These are refunds made on a case-by-case basis. Some examples of reasons we would honor a refund would include:

  • Accidental payment: if you paid for a yearly subscription instead of a month, we can help to revert your payment to a monthly plan. This would include a partial refund.

  • Accidental/unattended sign up: If you started a subscription but didn't use it at all. This can be tricky to determine; but if there are zero songs in your account and we see one or more payments, we're likely to assist with a refund.

  • Product malfunction: If you experience significant downtime due to an outage or feature that isn't working, we'll go above and beyond to offer credits to make up for it. Unlike monthly credits, these will carry over to the next month!

Some cases where we would not honor a refund:

  • Changed your mind: You've used Suno for six months, and decided now that you don't like it. In this case, our team will recommend you cancel your plan. You can continue to use the service through the end of the billing cycle, and the account will revert to the Basic plan upon completion.

    • If it's only been a day or two, give our team a shout at support@suno.com and we'll do our best to make it right!

  • Intermittent usage: If you've had a monthly plan and used Suno on and off for several months, we cannot issue a refund for the time you didn't use it. Instead, we encourage you to cancel anytime you won't be using Suno, and resubscribe when you're ready to use more than the Basic plan credit allotment!

How do I cancel my subscription

How do I cancel my subscription?

Edited

Have you made so many songs you forgot to go outside? Maybe you just need a quick break from all of the mind-blowing music you've made. Either way, you can cancel your subscription with these quick steps:

Tap your profile icon, then tap Subscription

Under Manage Subscription, scroll past Billing & Payment then tap Cancel Plan

One last step! Tap Confirm Cancellation to end your subscription at the end of the current billing period

If you have any trouble canceling, reach our team at support@suno.com and we'll be there to help!

How do I change my billing details

How do I change my billing details?

Edited

Need to update a payment method or change your address? Use the steps below to update your billing details!

Tap your profile icon and choose Subscription

In the window that appears, tap Edit Billing Details within the Billing & Payment field

This will take you to a new page from our payment partners at Stripe. You'll see your plan type, an editable payment method, and a button to cancel your plan.

Under Payment Method, you can tap on +Add payment method if you'd like to add another option. Click the triple-dot icon to select a default payment method or delete a payment on file (if you have multiple).

Scroll down to find Billing Information, then choose Update Information if you need to make any changes!

What happens if I use all of my paid credits

What happens if I use all of my paid credits?

Edited

If you're using a Pro or Premier plan, credits are replenished once monthly at the same time as your original purchase. For example, if you subscribed on July 4th at 3pm local time, your next replenishment will happen at 3pm local time on August 4th.

If you run out of monthly credits, you will receive the same 50 credit daily allotment as our Basic plan users, starting on the next day and running to the end of the billing cycle. When a new cycle begins, your monthly allotment will be used first, and the 50 daily credits will not reach your account until all monthly credits are used.

Note: Songs made with the 50 credits per day will still be granted commercial use rights, as long as you are still subscribed. Once your membership reverts to the Basic tier, any new songs will not be eligible for commercial use.

When will I get more credits

When will I get more credits?

Edited

OK, you've used your credits for the day... or the month! But when are they going to be replenished? The answer might be different than you expect.

If you're using the Basic (free) plan, up to 50 credits are replenished once daily. This currently uses Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) to ensure distribution is always the same.

If you're using a Pro or Premier plan, credits are replenished once monthly. Unlike the Basic plan, this will refresh at the same time as your original purchase. If you purchased on January 2nd at 3pm local time, your next replenishment will also happen at 3pm local time! Once you use all of your monthly credits, you will receive the same 50 credit daily allotment as our Basic plan users.

If you subscribed on the 31st of a month, your replenishment date will follow the last day of the month (the 31st when applicable, otherwise the 30th, or the 28th in February) If you don't see credits within 24 hours of the time stated above, reach our team at support@suno.com so we can help!
