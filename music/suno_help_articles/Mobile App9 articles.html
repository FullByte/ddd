Create Music with an Image

Create Music with an Image

Edited

Feeling inspired by what you see? Take a picture or use an photo from your camera roll to create a 30-second song with Suno Scenes!

Here's how to use Suno Scenes in a few easy steps:

  • Open Suno, tap Create, then toggle Custom to On.

  • Select Camera, and get your subject in frame!

  • Take the photo, then add a description in the text box.

  • Tap Create!

Suno will create two 30-second generations. The first is in view - drag up to find the second song!

Create Music with Audio

Create Music with Audio

Edited

Sing a tune and let Suno do the rest!

Here's how to use Suno to make a song from an audio clip:

  • Open Suno, tap Create, then toggle Custom to On.

  • With Audio selected, tap the button to start recording!

  • Tap again to stop, and your audio will upload automatically (saving a copy in your Library, too!)

  • Add a description and click Create!

Suno will create two 30-second generations. The first is in view - drag up to find the second song!

Create Music with Video

Create Music with Video

Edited

If a picture is worth a thousand words, what about a video? Using Suno Scenes, tap and hold to capture a video, or find one in your camera roll to create a 30-second song!

Here's how to use Suno Scenes with a video in a few easy steps:

  • Open Suno, tap Create, then toggle Custom to On.

  • With Camera selected, hold the button to record a video, or locate a video in your camera roll.

  • Crop the video duration if needed, then tap Save.

  • Add a description, then tap Create; Suno will let you know when your songs are ready!

Suno will create two 30-second generations. The first is in view; drag up to find the second song!

How do I delete my account

How do I delete my account?

Edited

You can delete your Suno account with just a few short taps!

  1. After opening the Suno app, tap the three horizontal lines at the top left of the homepage

  2. In the menu that appears, tap Account

  3. Located under the user details, tap Delete account

  4. Tap Yes, I'm sure to delete the account.

Deleting an account is a permanent action. Songs and any personal details will not be recoverable. If you need help before deleting, please reach support@suno.com for assistance!

How do I log in

How do I log in?

Edited

After downloading the Suno app, there are two options at the bottom of the Welcome page.

  • Tap the black button to create a free account.

  • If you already have an account, tap Sign In at the bottom right, directly below the black button.

Suno has two primary login methods; Sign in via Phone Number, or Connected Login.

  • Tap the white button to sign in using your phone number.

    • Enter your region code and phone number then tap Confirm. A verification code will be sent to the number via SMS

    • Enter the verification code and Suno will validate the code automatically.

  • Tap any of the four icons (Apple, Google, Microsoft, or Discord) and follow the steps you would normally use to login.

    • This method may require you to enter a password. In this case, enter the password you have already established to log into the service you selected.

Edited
