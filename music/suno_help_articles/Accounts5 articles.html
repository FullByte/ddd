How do I change my account login

How do I change my account login?

Edited

There's a quick process that requires just a few steps to update your login details!

First, log into your account using the same method you always have. If you try using another login type at this stage, this will create a new account!

Then, tap your profile icon and choose Manage Account.

From here, you can add a phone number or another connected account type. Once you have more than one login method added, use the dropdown tab at the far right to remove any login details you no longer need.

How do I change my Display Name

How do I change my Display Name?

Edited

Did you notice we add a placeholder display name when you create your account? You can change it anytime by following these steps:

Tap your profile icon and choose Edit Profile

Then, scroll down to Display Name and enter your preferred name.

Tap Save to update your profile!

Your Display Name does not need to match your Handle, but you might find it helpful for consistency. If they match, the name shown with your songs (and in playlists made by yourself, other users, or Suno!) will also be the name in your URL!

How do I change my Profile Avatar

How do I change my Profile Avatar?

Edited

You can change your profile avatar at any time. Replace it with an upload of your own, or let Suno generate an image for you!

Tap your profile icon and choose Edit Profile

In the menu that appears, you can tap to upload an image, or type a prompt and tap Generate for Suno to create a new image

Tap Save to update your avatar!

Uploaded images can be up to 10 MB, but be mindful that your image will cropped to round in certain areas!

What type of plan do I have

What type of plan do I have?

Edited

Suno offers three plan types, two of which are paid subscriptions.

Basic is our free tier, which currently offers 50 credits that replenish daily. This is enough to make 10 songs per day!

Pro is the entry option for paid subscriptions. With a Pro subscription, you receive an allotment of 2500 credits each month.

Premier is our highest credit-per-month plan available. With a Premier plan, you receive 10,000 credits each month.

There are some additional perks to your paid subscription, too! Check out https://suno.com/account for the latest pricing and plan features.

Need more credits? Pro and Premier plans also have the option to purchase more credits. Once you're signed up, you'll see the Buy More Credits button in the area linked above.

Pro and Premier users who reach their monthly allotment will now receive up to 50 credits per day after all monthly credits have been used!
